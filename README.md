This software is an image correction tool developed based on OpenCV. Its core functions are as follows: the deep learning algorithm automatically locates 4 feature points in each of the left and right regions of the image, performs perspective correction on the two regions respectively, corrects the tilted and deformed regions into regular rectangles, automatically saves the corrected images of the two regions after correction, splices them left and right into a complete image, and records the pixel coordinates of all 8 feature points for subsequent reuse and verification.
